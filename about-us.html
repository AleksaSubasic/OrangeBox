Online shopping companies, also known as e-commerce businesses, have grown in popularity in recent years as more and more consumers turn to the internet to purchase goods and services. With an online store, customers can shop at any time of the day, from anywhere with an internet connection, and have their purchases delivered right to their doorstep. Online shopping companies can specialize in a particular category of products, such as clothing or electronics, or they may offer a wide variety of goods. Online stores have changed how people shop.

Iko Makarume established OrangeBox in 1997. The orange emphasis refers to Iko Makarume's hometown of Kyoto. The goal behind it all was to make it easy to locate and purchase the products you require. In the beginning, OrangeBox started with a small selection of products, focusing mainly on electronics and home appliances. The company quickly gained a reputation for offering high-quality products at affordable prices, which helped it to attract a growing number of customers. As the company's customer base grew, so did its product offerings.

We are mostly known for high-quality products and excellent customer service. Our easy-to-use website allows customers to browse and purchase products with just a few clicks. OrangeBox offers a variety of payment options including credit cards, debit cards, net banking, and cash on delivery. We also offer free shipping on all orders above a certain amount and have a hassle-free return and exchange policy. Customers can contact our devoted team of customer service specialists with any queries or issues they may have at any time, 24/7.
